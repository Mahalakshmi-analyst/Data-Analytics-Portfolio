# 🍔 Food Delivery Platform Analysis
### Customer · Orders · Delivery · Restaurant Intelligence

## 📌 Project Overview

This end-to-end data analytics and machine learning project analyzes a food delivery platform to understand customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, and business growth opportunities.

The project combines SQL, Python, Excel, Power BI, Statistical Analysis, and Machine Learning to transform raw operational data into actionable business insights. It covers the complete analytics workflow—from data collection and cleaning to dashboard development, hypothesis testing, predictive modeling, and business recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to understand customer preferences and demand patterns in order to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer satisfaction, optimize restaurant performance, and maximize business profitability.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Study monthly order and revenue trends.
- Compare delivery performance across regions and vehicle types.
- Identify high-performing restaurants and delivery partners.
- Evaluate payment methods and customer loyalty.
- Perform hypothesis testing to validate business assumptions.
- Build machine learning models for prediction and customer segmentation.
- Generate dashboards for business decision-making.
- Provide data-driven business recommendations.
