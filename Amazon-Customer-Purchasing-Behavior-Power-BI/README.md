# 🛒 Amazon Customer Purchasing Behavior Analysis

**Course:** Exploratory Data Analysis Using Power BI

**Developed By:** L. Mahalakshmi

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior on Amazon using Power BI. The objective is to understand customer preferences, revenue patterns, purchasing trends, and promotion effectiveness through interactive dashboards and data visualizations.

The analysis places special emphasis on Non-Prime customers to identify opportunities for Prime membership conversion, improve customer engagement, and increase overall business revenue.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

The goal of this project is to analyze customer purchasing behavior on Amazon.

The project focuses on understanding revenue patterns, customer preferences, promotional effectiveness, and the purchasing behavior of Prime and Non-Prime customers.

The insights generated from this analysis can help improve marketing strategies, customer retention, and business profitability.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchase behavior to understand the differences between Prime and Non-Prime customers.
- Identify high-performing product categories, subcategories, and brands that generate the highest revenue from delivered orders.
- Evaluate the impact of promotional offers and seasonal trends on customer purchasing behavior and overall revenue.
- Identify active Non-Prime customers with high purchase activity who can be targeted for Prime membership conversion.
- Generate data-driven recommendations to encourage Non-Prime customers to upgrade to Prime and improve overall business revenue.
